Victory City Under Construction

This is a view of the rear of a Victory City under construction. As you look up the highway, the industry buildings are to the left and the cafeteria buildings are to the right.

This drawing was conceived and drawn by fifth-year architectural student, Tom Sheehan, who has since graduated from the University of Cincinnati.
