ven though Victory City is presently just an elaborate collection of maps, details and floor plans, inventor Orville Simpson II hopes the fictious "city of the future" described on this website will actually be under construction by the year 2002.

For more detailed information about Victory City, be sure to visit the Residents Guide, which more fully describes the overall concept of Victory City, as well as what it might be like to live and work there.
