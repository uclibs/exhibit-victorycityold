Fire Escape for Family Cart Area of Cafeteria

The fire escape would project outward from the Cafeteria Building. The structure would feature both conventional stairs and a wide, circular ramp descending gently downward, which would enable little children and parents carrying babies to descend safely.

See also: Fire Escape located on the Map of a Victory City and the Safety and Security section of the Residents Guide.
