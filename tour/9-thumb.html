Platform Movement Chart for Extra-Large Elevators (ELE's)

This chart shows the sequence of patterns resulting from the platform movements of the extra-large elevators, which would carry up to 50 people at one time. Notice the first and last positions are identical, and all the other positions illustrate how many movements these elevators would have to go through to get back to their original positions.

Illustration by Orville Simpson II.
