If you're interested in learning more about urban planning and city architecture, the websites listed below are chock-full of good information.

  • Arcosanti Project — An experimental, three-dimensional, pedestrian-oriented city in the high desert of Arizona.
  • Best Practices Database — Solutions to common urban problems facing the world's cities today.
  • carfree.com: Designs for Carfree Cities — Car-free cities past, present, and future. Solutions to the problem of the urban automobile.
  • Fuller, Buckminster — Inventor of the Geodesic Dome and other architectural wonders.
  • Rivertown Simulation — An experimental interactive simulation of downtown revitalization in the fictional Rivertown.
  • Sustainable Urban Design and Climate — Paper which outlines how climate sensitive design leads to more sustainable cities.
  • Urban Planning, 1794-1918 — An international anthology of articles, conference papers, and reports.
  • Wright, Frank Lloyd — One of the greatest architects and artists of all time.
